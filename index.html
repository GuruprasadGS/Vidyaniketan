About Our School

Nurturing Young Minds
Since Decades

Karnataka State Syllabus — Affiliated School

Vidyaniketan Education Trust is committed to providing quality education that nurtures the intellectual, emotional, and social growth of every child. Located in the heart of Afzalpur, Kalaburagi, we have been serving the community with dedication and excellence — offering English Medium education from LKG through 10th Standard.

Academic Excellence

Following the Karnataka State Syllabus, our curriculum ensures students are well-prepared for board examinations while developing critical thinking and a lifelong love of learning.

Holistic Development

Beyond academics, we foster character, creativity, and confidence through sports, cultural activities, and educational excursions across Karnataka.

Community Rooted

Proudly serving the Afzalpur community in Kalaburagi district, we empower local children with world-class English medium education close to home.
